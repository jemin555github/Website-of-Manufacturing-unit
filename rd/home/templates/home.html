Rising the bars of standard in Brass products

R D Brass Industries was established in the year 1995 to set precedents in the area of extrusions of non-ferrous metals. The unit is set up at Jamnagar, the city known as "Brass City of India". Today, the persistent demand for our products, which has enhanced our enormous manufacturing capacity, confirms our corporate vision. We manufacture Precision Metal Components, brass CNC machined components, brass sanitary parts, brass forged components, brass electrical components, brass and carbon steel fasteners, various types of Copper Alloy Extrusions and Brass Extrusions, which includes Copper/Brass Rod, Ingot, Billet, Various Profiles & Sections in conformation to different standards like IS, BS, DIN, ASTM, etc. Our division is accredited with the honor of ISO 9001: 2015 certification
